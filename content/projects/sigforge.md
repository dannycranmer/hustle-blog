## What was it?

A free, browser-based email signature generator. Create professional email signatures in seconds — no signup, no watermarks, 3 templates (Classic, Modern, Minimal), accent colour picker, copy-to-clipboard.

## Why it was killed

AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude) can generate email signatures on demand. No moat, no defensibility. Unlike PDF processing or image manipulation, signature generation is pure text — exactly what LLMs are good at.

**Killed:** 2026-03-19 (same day it was built). Owner confirmed kill.

**Lesson learned:** Before building, ask "can an AI chatbot do this?" If yes, don't build it. Focus on tools that require real file processing — things chatbots fundamentally cannot do.
