## What is it?

Free, browser-based educational games for kids aged 4–12. Math, phonics, spelling, and sight words — all running 100% client-side. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection. COPPA-safe by design because there's nothing to comply with.

**Why it exists:** ABCya just went paywalled at $79/yr. CoolMathGames uses behavioral ads that track children. IXL has a 1.2/5 on Trustpilot. Parents and teachers deserve free, private, quality educational games.

**Why AI can't replace it:** Kids need interactive, visual, game-based practice — not a chat interface. You can't gamify multiplication drills in ChatGPT.

## Games (11)

- **Multiplication Tables** — pick a table (2×–12×) or mix, 10 multiple-choice questions with streak tracking
- **Addition & Subtraction** — three difficulty levels (to 10 / to 50 / to 100), type-in answers
- **Number Bonds** — visual circle layout, find the missing part, multiple choice
- **Times Tables Speed Challenge** — 60-second timer, type answers as fast as possible
- **Counting & Skip Counting** — count objects, what comes next, skip counting by 2s/5s/10s (ages 4-6)
- **Fractions Introduction** — name fractions, compare fractions, equivalent fractions with visual bar models
- **Phonics — Letter Sounds** — match letters to words, consonants/vowels/blends & digraphs with emoji hints. Audio via Web SpeechSynthesis.
- **Spelling Practice** — see an emoji, type the spelling, 3 levels across 5 word categories
- **Sight Words** — Dolch & Fry flash cards, Pre-K through Grade 2, timed & untimed modes
- **Progress persistence** — localStorage tracks per-game stats, gold stars on completed levels, homepage badges

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML/CSS/JS. Nunito font, bright playful colours, fully mobile responsive. GitHub Pages.
