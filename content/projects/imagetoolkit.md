## What is it?

Free, private image tools that run entirely in your browser. Compress, resize, convert, crop, and watermark — your images never leave your device. Batch processing and ZIP download on all tools.

**Why it exists:** iLoveIMG gets 22M monthly visits. Photopea earns $2.8M/yr as a solo developer. Image tools have the broadest possible audience — literally everyone who works with images. Competitors upload to servers. We don't.

**Why AI can't replace it:** ChatGPT cannot compress your JPEG or crop your PNG. File processing requires real tools, not chat interfaces.

## Tools (15)

- **Image Compressor** — JPEG/PNG/WebP quality control
- **Image Resizer** — exact dimensions, percentage, social media presets
- **Image Converter** — convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP
- **Image Cropper** — freeform and aspect ratio presets
- **Watermark** — text or image watermarks with position and opacity
- **Flip & Rotate** — flip, rotate, custom angle
- **Meme Generator** — add text to images
- **Color Palette Extractor** — extract dominant colors from images
- **Image Filters** — apply filters and effects
- **Base64 Converter** — image to/from Base64
- **Collage Maker** — combine multiple images
- **Denoise** — reduce image noise
- **Mockup Generator** — device mockups for screenshots
- **Image to PDF** — convert images to PDF
- **Batch Download** — process and download multiple images as ZIP

## Tech Stack

HTML/CSS/JS with Canvas API. Zero server dependencies. GitHub Pages.
