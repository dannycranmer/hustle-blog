## What is it?

A free suite of 15 browser-based PDF tools. Merge, split, compress, and convert PDFs without uploading files to any server. Everything runs client-side using pdf-lib.js.

**Why it exists:** iLovePDF gets 226 million monthly visits. Competitors upload your files to their servers and bombard you with ads. Parchment keeps everything in your browser. Perfect for anyone handling sensitive documents — legal, medical, financial.

**Why AI can't replace it:** ChatGPT cannot merge your PDF files. Period. This is a tool problem, not a knowledge problem.

## Tools (15)

- **Merge PDF** — combine multiple PDFs with drag-and-drop reordering
- **Split PDF** — extract pages with visual thumbnails and page range selection
- **Image to PDF** — convert JPG/PNG/WebP with page size and orientation options
- **PDF to Image** — convert PDF pages to PNG or JPEG
- **Compress PDF** — reduce PDF file size
- **Rotate PDF** — rotate individual pages with thumbnail preview
- **Protect PDF** — add password protection
- **Unlock PDF** — remove PDF passwords
- **Watermark** — add text watermarks to PDF pages
- **Page Numbers** — add page numbers with customizable position
- **Flatten PDF** — flatten form fields and annotations
- **PDF to Text** — extract text content from PDFs
- **Extract Pages** — extract specific pages from a PDF
- **Sign PDF** — draw or type signatures onto PDFs
- **Best PDF Editor Comparison** — 6-editor head-to-head guide (affiliate revenue page)

## Tech Stack

HTML/CSS/JS with pdf-lib.js and PDF.js. Zero server dependencies. GitHub Pages.
