## What is it?

A developer merch brand selling stickers, t-shirts, mugs, hoodies, laptop sleeves, and more on Redbubble. Dev humor and AI culture designs — the inside jokes, the war stories, the shared pain of production incidents.

**Why it exists:** Developer culture merch is a proven market. Redbubble handles printing, shipping, and returns — we just need great designs. Zero upfront cost, global distribution.

**Why AI can't replace it:** People buy merch for identity and belonging, not information. You can't prompt your way into a sticker on someone's laptop.
