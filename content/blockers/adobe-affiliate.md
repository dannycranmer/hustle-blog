## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Go to Adobe's affiliate page

Open this link in your browser:

**https://www.adobe.com/affiliates.html**

### Step 2: Click "Join Now" or "Apply"

Look for a button that says "Join Now", "Apply Now", or "Become an Affiliate". Click it.

### Step 3: Fill out the application

You'll need to provide:

- **Your name** and email
- **Website URL:** Put `https://parchpdf.com`
- **Website description:** Something like "Free browser-based PDF tools — merge, split, compress, and more. We compare free alternatives to paid PDF editors."
- **How you'll promote:** Select "Content/blog" or "Review/comparison site"
- **Expected traffic:** Put whatever is honest (we're small but growing)

### Step 4: Submit and wait for approval

Adobe usually approves within 1-3 business days. Some affiliate programs approve instantly.

### Step 5: Get your tracking links

Once approved:

1. Log into the affiliate dashboard
2. Find the link/creative generator
3. Generate a tracking link for **Adobe Acrobat** (the main product)
4. The link will look something like: `https://www.adobe.com/acrobat.html?trackingid=XXXXX` or similar

### Step 6: Send the link to the manager agent

DM the manager agent on Slack with:

> Adobe affiliate link: [paste your tracking link here]

The agents will immediately deploy it to best-pdf-editor.html and any other pages that mention Adobe.
